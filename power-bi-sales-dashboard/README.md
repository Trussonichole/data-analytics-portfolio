# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard (2024–2026)

## 🔍 Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes sales performance across products, time, and customer segments. It is designed to help stakeholders understand revenue trends, identify top-performing products, and explore sales distribution across categories.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Revenue shows an overall upward trend with peaks in late 2026
* A small number of products drive the majority of total sales
* Laptops account for over 50% of total revenue
* Customer base is relatively small compared to order volume, indicating repeat purchases

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Power BI (Data Visualization)
* DAX (Calculated Measures)
* Excel (Data Preparation)

## 📊 Features

* KPI cards for Total Orders, Customers, and Revenue
* Time-series analysis of sales trends
* Product-level performance breakdown
* Category-level revenue distribution
* Interactive filters for:

  * Date range
  * Country
  * Product category
